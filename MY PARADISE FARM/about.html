PARADISE FARM HISTORY

OUR MISSION

To deliver fresh, affordable farm produce to Kiambu families within 24 hours of harvest. We use drip irrigation and organic methods to keep food healthy and prices fair. We train local youth in agribusiness and support sustainable farming.

OUR VISSION

To be Kiambu’s most trusted source of healthy, locally grown food. We aim to expand greenhouse farming for year-round supply. We envision every family accessing nutritious food grown close to home

Paradise Farm is a diversified agribusiness specializing in:


1.Dairy
2.Horticulture
3.Fruits
4.Flowers
5.Greenhouse and plantation farming.

Our History

Founded 2020 in Kiambururu, Kiambu. Started with 1/4 acre of sukuma wiki. Added dairy cows in 2021 and greenhouses in 2022. Today we farm 3 acres with dairy, vegetables, and fruits, employing local youth.
